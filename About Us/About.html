About Us

About

APU Astronomical Club(AAC) is mainly in English to communicate. It was started in 2014 by a group of astronomy enthusiasts in an amateur way.

Mission

Provide a good communication platform for APU student who are an astronomy enthusiasts or even a beginners, promote astronomy education and increase APUs' student awareness of astronomy.

Organization

The management of the APU Astronomical Club is elected annually through the General Assembly. There are 4 committees which are the President, Vice President, Club Assitance and Club Tresurer. At the same time, we have also set up three groups, namely the activity group, the photography group and the webpage group in order to grow the club.

Member

APU student who are interested in astronomy with an annual fee of RM30. Currently more than 20 members in AAC.

Funds

Funding comes from membership fees, school event charity fund, sponsorship by school outsider enthusiasts and groups.

Event

Every year, at least one two-day and one-night star-gazing seminar will be held to enhance the relation between every members. In addition, there will be an average of more than 10 events per year for members to increase their interest in learning astronomy.
